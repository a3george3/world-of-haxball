Haxball Pro Settings and Gear List

Welcome to our HaxBall Pro Settings and Gear List. This is where we get our data to give you our analysis of the most used gaming peripherals and gear and our competitive settings guide. We research everything we can find, from settings like Haxball Zoom & Browser Zoom, extrapolation, low latency and resolution to gear like monitors and keyboards.

Maybe you don’t want to know what the average resolution of professional players is, but are much more interested in what sensitivity your favorite HaxBall pros like ^aMp^, Kyo, Al1s, Bachira, nik, Hakut, modric, Enzo, or supermazhine are using. In that case, you’ve come to the right place. We have all the top teams in competitive HaxBall and listed them in the list below. When applicable, you can click on a player’s name in order to see more detailed data on the player (stuff like their Haxball goals, assists, team history and so on).

The list you see below is connected to our database where we update the information as soon as possible. If we made any mistakes or you see any outdated information, please feel free to join us in the comments to discuss these settings and their sources.
