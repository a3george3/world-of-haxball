WHAT IS WORLDOFHAXBALL.COM

The Global Hub Connecting All HaxBall Communities Worldwide.

WorldOfHaxBall.com is a central platform designed to connect Europe, North America, South America, and Asia into one unified HaxBall ecosystem. Our goal is to serve as the main portal where players can discover major leagues, join tournaments, track statistics, and stay connected with the global competitive scene — all in one place.
